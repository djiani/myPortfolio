Cameroon Research Platform (Live source code)

Technologies Used:

  • Html5/CSS/Boostrap/JQuery
  • Nodejs, Express
  • Mongodb/mongoose
  • Mocha/Chai

Project description

As today, There is not a common platform where you can find cameroon professors or african professors, except those who teach out of the continent. And therefore, it is more challenging to find an African project with to collaborate on project or to simply know what there are working on. Cameroon Research Platform is a web platform design in the first place to share and promote Cameroonian professors but also, african professors and all university profossors and researchers who would like to collaborate with others professors around the world or who would like to share its research interest. We encourage all cameroonian professors, all african professors, or any professor to register to this app and upload his cv, a short description about himselft, and fill out a summary about his research insterest in other to build a strong network.
